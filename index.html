About

Victors FC is a student-run women's soccer team at the University of Michigan. We offer students the opportunity to play soccer at a competitive level against college club teams with a smaller time commitment than U of M Club Soccer and in a more competitive environment than IM soccer.

In the fall, we practice twice a week and have 1-2 games on the weekend (never on football Saturdays). Our first season was Fall 2017. Last year we placed first in our league!

We are excited to continue to play this year, even though the season will be substantially modified due to COVID-19 restrictions.
